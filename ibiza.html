FAQ — Nightlife in Ibiza

What are the best nightclubs in Ibiza? ▾
The top clubs include [UNVRS] (DJ Mag #1 World 2026), Hï Ibiza (4× DJ Mag #1), Pacha (since 1973), Ushuaïa (open-air superclub), Amnesia, DC-10 (Circoloco), Club Chinois (Bedouin SAGA, Claptone), Cova Santa (countryside cave) and 528 Ibiza (Benimussa Hills).
When is the Ibiza club season? ▾
The Ibiza season typically runs from late May to early October, with opening parties in late May/early June and closing parties in September/October. Peak season is July and August. Most clubs are closed outside of the summer season.
What time do clubs open in Ibiza? ▾
Most Ibiza clubs open between 23:00 and midnight and close between 06:00 and 08:00. Ushuaïa is the exception, starting at 17:00 for its open-air concerts. Akasha and Cova Santa also run sunset garden sessions from 19:00.
What are the best areas for nightlife in Ibiza? ▾
Platja d'en Bossa for Hï Ibiza and Ushuaïa; San Rafael for [UNVRS] and Amnesia; Ibiza Town Marina for Pacha, Club Chinois and Lío; San Antonio for Eden, Es Paradis, O Beach, Soul City and Plastik; Santa Eulalia for Akasha; Benimussa Hills for 528 Ibiza; Las Salinas for DC-10.
How much does entry cost in Ibiza clubs? ▾
Entry ranges from €10 at Plastik to €15–35 at boutique clubs like Akasha and Club Chinois, up to €25–45 at the major venues. The world's biggest nights — [UNVRS], Hï Ibiza and Ushuaïa — can be €30–60+. Many clubs offer cheaper advance tickets via official sites or DICE.
