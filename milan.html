FAQ — Nightlife in Milan

What are the best nightclubs in Milan? ▾
The top clubs in Milan include The Club Milano (Corso Garibaldi 97), Hollywood Rythmoteque (Corso Como 15), Alcatraz (Via Valtellina 25), Gattopardo (Via Piero della Francesca 47) and Amnesia Milano. Each offers a different vibe — from luxury commercial to underground electronic.
What time do clubs open in Milan? ▾
Milan clubs typically open at 11pm but don't fill up until 1–2am. Most close between 5am and 6am. Arriving before 1am is generally not necessary and may result in a nearly empty venue.
How much does entry cost at Milan clubs? ▾
Entry prices in Milan range from €10 for smaller underground clubs to €25–30 for larger commercial venues. Many clubs offer free or discounted entry before midnight or with a guest list signup.
What is the best area for nightlife in Milan? ▾
Corso Como and Corso Garibaldi are the most concentrated nightlife areas with multiple clubs within walking distance. Zona Farini hosts larger warehouse-style venues like Alcatraz. Lambrate and Porta Romana have more underground options.
Are Milan clubs open all year? ▾
Yes — unlike coastal resorts, Milan clubs are open year-round, typically Thursday to Saturday nights. Some venues also host events on Sundays.
