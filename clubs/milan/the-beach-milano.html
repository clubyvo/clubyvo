The Beach Milano is a unique club and restaurant concept in Milan, bringing beach party vibes to the Lambrate district. Open Friday, Saturday and Sunday, it combines aperitivo and dinner with a full club night experience — making it the perfect venue for an evening that starts early and ends late.

The weekly programme spans Reggaeton parties, House nights, LGBTQ+ events and Karaoke evenings, offering something different every night. A large private parking is available for guests, and table booking is available online.
