Tunnel Club Milano is one of the most iconic underground clubs in Italy — literally. Built beneath the railway tracks of Milano Centrale station and open since 1995, it has offered Milanese clubbers a connection to all sounds of electronic music in an atmosphere that no other venue in the city can replicate.

The location is unique: the classic tunnel layout — with an entrance and exit leading into a new world — creates an immersive experience where, once inside, guests rarely want to leave. Over the years Tunnel has hosted famous national and international artists and remains a key piece of Milan's nightlife history.

Beyond club nights, Tunnel is a multifunctional venue available for private parties, corporate events, concerts, fashion shows, photo shoots and more.
