Destino Five Ibiza is a 5-star grand luxe resort perched on the cliffs of Cap Martinet, blending world-class hospitality with Ibiza's most iconic nightlife brand: Pacha. As the resort itself puts it, "Pacha is our muse, and Ibiza is our home." Guests staying at Destino enjoy free entry to Pacha nightclub, making it a true base camp for an Ibiza clubbing holiday with five-star comfort.

The centrepiece of the resort is Playa Pacha — the White Isle's iconic cherry-shaped pool club, set in a cliffside oasis complete with a social jacuzzi, Mediterranean bites, cocktails and soulful daytime DJ sets. During the day, Pacha Icons takes over the resort with an open-air experience combining sun-soaked beats, panoramic sea views and an elevated island-luxury crowd — described as "Ibiza days hit differently." The resort also features the cliffside restaurant Elia with Dalt Vila views and live DJ sets at golden hour, the all-day terrace restaurant Cielo, and is certified LEED Platinum for sustainability.
