Amnesia Ibiza is one of the most storied and culturally significant clubs in the world — the self-described "home of electronic music" and a true temple of the Ibiza spirit. Located on the main road between Ibiza Town and San Antonio in San Rafael, it has been at the forefront of the island's clubbing culture for decades, championing freedom, diversity and the power of music to unite people.

The club operates across two iconic spaces — the vast Main Room and the legendary open-air Terraza — hosting more than 20 distinct residencies and event brands per season. From the disco and house euphoria of Glitterbox to the hard techno of Teletech, the underground warmth of Cocoon and Do Not Sleep, the nostalgic power of Rememberland and the full-throttle energy of Resistance — Amnesia covers the entire spectrum of electronic music with uncompromising quality. España de Noche honoured Amnesia in 2026 for its legacy and contribution to nightlife culture.
