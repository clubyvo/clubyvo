Disco Tropics is one of the most famous nightclubs in Lloret de Mar and a major hotspot on the Costa Brava nightlife scene. Open since the 1970s, it has become a true institution of Spanish summer nightlife, attracting thousands of young tourists every season from across Europe.

The club features 3,000 capacity spread across three unique dance floors — each with its own sound and vibe. Expect impressive light shows, LED screens and international DJs performing live throughout the summer. Entry prices vary by night, and multi-night passes are available for those staying in Lloret for several days.
