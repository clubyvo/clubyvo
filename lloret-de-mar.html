FAQ — Nightlife in Lloret de Mar

What are the best clubs in Lloret de Mar? ▾
The main clubs in Lloret de Mar are Disco Tropics, St. Trop', Alcatraz, Revolution Disco, MOËF GAGA and Gala CCCP, all located in the main nightlife strip around Av. Just Marlés Vilarrodona and Carrer de Santa Cristina.
When is the best time to visit Lloret de Mar for nightlife? ▾
Peak season runs from late June to early September. July and August offer the full experience with all clubs open nightly. For shorter queues and lower prices, the first two weeks of June or last two weeks of September are ideal.
How much does entry cost in Lloret de Mar? ▾
Entry prices are generally affordable — typically €10 to €20, often including a drink. Many clubs offer free entry before 1am or with a guest list via their Instagram or website.
Are clubs in Lloret de Mar open in winter? ▾
Most clubs in Lloret de Mar are open summer season only (June–September). Outside this period, the majority are closed. If planning a winter trip specifically for nightlife, Barcelona or Madrid are better alternatives.
